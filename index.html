          About

          Why should I use SocialProof?
          You can use SocialProof to reach potential customers in your area via great reviews. Building your local presence and growing your business have never been easier or more affordable.


          Where will my ad show up?
          Your ad will be shown to Facebook users in your local area who need your services. It will appear right at the center of the Facebook News Feed and will look like something shared by a friend.


          How do you know which people need your services?
          SocialProof is a partner in the Facebook Ads API program. Our technology combines a variety of data sources—including income levels, employment information, and online behavior—to identify potential customers who need your services so that you can reach an audience of the highest possible value.


          How much does this cost?
          SocialProof believes that high-end marketing shouldn’t have a high-end price tag.
          We offer affordable marketing solutions for every budget. For just $29, you can reach 1,000 Facebook users in your area who need your services. We also offer a $49 plan that reaches 2,500 potential customers, a $79 plan that reaches 5,000 potential customers, and a $99 plan that reaches 7,500 potential customers. Every plan includes our custom targeting, ensuring that the potential customers you reach live in your local area and need your services.


          What happens when someone clicks on my ad?
          When someone clicks on your ad, they’ll be sent directly to a website of your choice where they can learn more about you. You can edit your website URL by clicking the blue 'Edit' button in the last step!


          How will I know how my ad is doing?
          When you join SocialProof, you'll receive a free 15 minute kick-off call with a SocialProof Marketing Specialist. They'll help set up your campaign so that it delivers maximum results for your business. From that moment on, SocialProof manage every aspect of your Facebook ad campaign. You'll receive email reports on your campaign that will help you see how your ad is doing and gain quick insight into your results.


          Is this month-to-month? Is there a contract?
          SocialProof offers a month-to-month subscription with no contracts or hidden fees. We believe in our product and don’t need to lock our customers in to keep them on board. In fact, about 95% of our customers continue running their ads after the first month--and it's because they love our service!

          Since it takes time to build up brand awareness in your area, we do encourage that you plan to stay with our service for at least several months to start seeing the full benefits. SocialProof is an investment that builds on itself—our most successful clients are those that have been with us the longest.

          In order to change or cancel your account, we ask that you call our customer line at 313-373-7835. Because we work hard to make our service as affordable and effective as possible, we are unable to offer refunds or discounts.
